Pancakes

Home

Description

Soft, fluffy pancakes that are a breakfast favorite around the world. These pancakes are easy to make from scratch and have a light, airy texture that pairs perfectly with sweet toppings like syrup, fruit or butter.

Adding fresh strawberries and banana slices makes them extra delicious, a treat everyone will love.

Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 200 g flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 300 ml milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 40 g melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • 100 g strawberries (sliced)
  • 2 bananas (sliced)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, sugar and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk milk, eggs, melted butter and the vanilla extract.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients until just mixed.
  4. Heat a pan over medium heat and lightly grease it.
  5. Pour batter into the pan to form pancakes.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, the flip.
  7. Cook the other side until golden brown.
  8. Arrange sliced stawberries and bananas on top of the pancakes.
  9. Serve warm with syrup, extra fruit or butter if desired.
