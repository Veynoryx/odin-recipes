Scrambled Eggs


Description

Creamy and tender scrambled eggs that are quick to prepare and full of flavor. This simple dish is a breakfast staple and can be customized to your liking. When cooked gently, the eggs become soft and rich, making them a perfect option for a warm, satisfying start to your day.

Serves 4

Prep time: 5 minutes
Cook time: 5 minutes
Difficulty: Very Easy

Ingredients

  • 8 eggs
  • 30 g butter
  • 60 ml milk (optional)
  • salt (to taste)
  • black pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl and whisk well.
  2. Add a splash of milk if you want creamier eggs.
  3. Heat butter in a pan over low to medium heat.
  4. Pour in the eggs.
  5. Stir gently as the eggs begin to cook.
  6. Continue stirring until eggs form soft curds and are slightly runny (they will finish cooking off the heat).
  7. Season with salt and pepper and serve immediately.
