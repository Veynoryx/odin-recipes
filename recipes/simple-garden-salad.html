Simple Garden Salad

Home

Description

A fresh, colorful salad made with crisp vegetables and a light, zesty dressing. This simple garden salad is refreshing, healthy, and incredibly easy to prepare. It works great as a side dish for almost any meal or as a quick, light option when you want something nutritious and satisfying.

Serves 4

Prep time: 5 minutes

Cook time: 0 minutes

Difficulty: Very Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 large head of lettuce (or 2 small)
  • 3 medium tomatos
  • 1 large cumcumber
  • 1/2 red onion
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1-2 tablespoons lemon juice or vinegar
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Black pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Wash and prepare all vegetables.
  2. Place lettuce in a large bowl.
  3. Add tomatoes, cucumber and red onion.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice or vinegar.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Toss everythiing together gently.
  7. Serve immediately.
